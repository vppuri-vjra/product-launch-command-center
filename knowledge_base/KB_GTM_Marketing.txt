--- COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE ---

Competitor A (ShopFast):
  Loyalty program: Points-based, no tiers, 1 point = $0.005 (half our value)
  Checkout: 3-step, no guest checkout
  Weakness: No Apple/Google Pay; poor mobile UX scores (App Store: 3.8)
  Our advantage: Better points value, tier perks, modern checkout

Competitor B (BuyNow):
  Loyalty program: Cashback model (1% on all purchases)
  Checkout: One-page but requires account — no guest
  Weakness: No iOS widget; cashback limited to store credit
  Our advantage: True guest checkout; flexible payment methods; push milestone engagement

GTM Response Playbook (for Sales team):
Q: "ShopFast also has a loyalty program — why is yours better?"
A: "Our points are worth 2x more per dollar. And our Gold tier perks — free shipping plus dedicated support — aren't something they offer at all."

Q: "BuyNow has one-page checkout too."
A: "They do, but you can't check out as a guest. We removed that barrier entirely. Plus we support Apple Pay and Google Pay — BuyNow doesn't."
