# Project Nightingale — Product Launch Plan
## Mobile App v3.0: Redesigned Checkout + Loyalty Program
## Launch Date: April 15, 2026

---

## Launch Overview
Project Nightingale is the launch of our mobile app version 3.0, which includes two major features: a completely redesigned checkout experience and a new customer loyalty program called "Perks." This is our biggest product launch of the year. The launch will be a staged rollout: 5% of users on April 15, 25% on April 18, 50% on April 22, and 100% on April 25.

---

## Feature: Redesigned Checkout
- One-tap checkout for returning customers with saved payment methods
- Guest checkout option (no account required)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay support
- Real-time delivery estimate shown before payment
- Address auto-complete powered by Google Places API
- Estimated conversion rate improvement: 18-22% based on A/B testing during beta
- Beta test results: 4,200 users tested over 3 weeks. Average checkout time dropped from 72 seconds to 38 seconds. Cart abandonment rate dropped from 34% to 19%.

## Feature: Loyalty Program ("Perks")
- Points earned on every purchase: 1 point per dollar spent
- Tier system: Bronze (0-499 points), Silver (500-1499 points), Gold (1500+ points)
- Silver perks: Free shipping on orders over $25, early access to sales
- Gold perks: Free shipping on all orders, exclusive products, birthday bonus (500 points)
- Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity
- Integration with existing user accounts (no separate sign-up needed)
- Projected enrollment: 40% of active users within first 90 days based on competitive benchmarks

## Technical Readiness

### Infrastructure
- Load testing completed: system handles 10x normal traffic without degradation
- CDN caching optimized for new product images and loyalty program assets
- Database migration for loyalty points table completed and verified March 20
- Rollback plan documented: if critical issues arise, revert to v2.9 within 15 minutes using feature flags

### Known Issues at Launch
1. Minor: Loyalty points balance may take up to 60 seconds to update after a purchase (async processing). Documented in FAQ.
2. Minor: Address auto-complete does not work for PO Box addresses. Manual entry required. Fix planned for v3.1 (May release).
3. Cosmetic: On tablets in landscape mode, the Perks dashboard has a slight layout shift on first load. Fix in v3.0.1 hotfix (April 17).

### Monitoring
- Real-time dashboard set up in Datadog for: checkout conversion rate, payment failure rate, API response times, loyalty enrollment rate
- Alerting configured: PagerDuty alerts if payment failure rate exceeds 2% or API p99 latency exceeds 500ms
- War room scheduled: April 15, 8am-6pm ET. Core team in Slack channel #nightingale-launch

## Risk Register

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Stripe payment failures spike on launch day | Medium | High | Pre-warmed Stripe account, fallback to PayPal for failed transactions |
| App store review process delays the update | Low | High | Submitted update on April 8 (7 days buffer). Expedited review requested. |
| Loyalty points calculation error | Low | Medium | Extensive unit testing (98% coverage). Manual audit of first 1000 transactions. |
| Negative press due to bugs | Medium | High | Beta testing with 4,200 users already completed. Known issues documented. Rapid hotfix process in place. |
| Support team overwhelmed | Medium | Medium | 4 temp agents hired. Auto-responder for top FAQs. Escalation path documented. |
| Competitor launches similar feature same week | Low | Low | Our beta data proves product-market fit. Accelerate PR timeline if needed. |
